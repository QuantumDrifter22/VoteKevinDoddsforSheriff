About Kevin Dodds

Kevin Dodds is a dedicated law enforcement professional and community advocate, passionate about making a positive difference in the lives of those he serves. With over fourty years of experience in law enforcement, including time as a chief in Pennsylvania and service with the Dallas Police Department, Kevin has a proven record of leadership, integrity, and commitment to justice.

Kevin’s journey in public service began in 1978 as an Auxillary Police Officer in Pennsylvania, where he worked tirelessly to uphold the safety and security of his community. His experience on the front lines has given him a deep understanding of the challenges facing law enforcement today, and he is committed to fostering trust and collaboration between officers and the community. Known for his proactive approach, Kevin focuses on preventive measures, community engagement, and transparent communication as the cornerstone of his philosophy.

In addition to his law enforcement role, Kevin is actively involved in several local communities and educational partners. His passion for learning reflects his dedication to bringing modern solutions and innovative thinking to law enforcement. He believes in leveraging technology to improve efficiency, enhance communication, and ensure that public safety strategies are responsive to the needs of a changing world.

Kevin’s campaign for Sheriff is centered on three guiding principles: safety, service, and integrity. He is committed to creating a safe environment for all, supporting his team with the best training and resources, and ensuring transparency in all operations. A firm believer in accountability and community partnership, Kevin Dodds aims to lead by example, setting high standards for himself and his team.

Outside of his work, Kevin cherishes time with his family, and he brings this sense of responsibility and care to every aspect of his role. He understands the trust placed in him by his community, and he is honored to serve with humility , respect, and dedication.

Join Kevin Dodds in building a safer, stronger community—together.
